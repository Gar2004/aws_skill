Amazon Alexa Ethical Families Skill



The Alexa skill "Ethical Families" serves as a brief introduction to the ethical categories of person, happiness, relationships and virtues. Once downloaded to your Alexa device activate the skill by saying "Alex, open ethical stuff". Once the skill is opened Alexa will give a brief introduction to the ethical families and prompt you to chose an ethical family to learn about. For more information please watch the below video.
